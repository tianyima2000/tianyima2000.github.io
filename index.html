About me

I am a PhD student at the Statistical Laboratory, University of Cambridge, supervised by Prof Richard Samworth and Prof Tengyao Wang. Previously, I obtained my MMath and BA degrees in Mathematics from the University of Cambridge in July 2023. This year, I am also organising the Statistics Clinic.

Research interests

I am broadly interested in statistics and machine learning, with a particular focus on nonparametric statistics, deep learning theory, and missing data problems.
