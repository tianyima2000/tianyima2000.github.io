About me

I am a third-year PhD student at the Statistical Laboratory, University of Cambridge, supervised by Prof Richard Samworth and Prof Tengyao Wang. Previously, I obtained my MMath and BA degrees in Mathematics from the University of Cambridge in July 2023.

Research interests

I am broadly interested in statistics and machine learning. In particular, my research focuses on nonparametric statistics and theories for deep learning and generative AI.
